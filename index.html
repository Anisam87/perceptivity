11 Questions

Before you book.

What's the difference between SEO and AEO/GEO?
SEO optimises for a list of ten blue links. AEO and GEO optimise for a single AI-generated answer. When a buyer asks ChatGPT or Gemini, the engine reads the web, forms a verdict, and names one brand. Rank 6 on Google doesn't appear in that answer at all.
How is this different from AEO monitoring tools?
Monitoring tools report that AI mentioned you — a lagging signal. Perceptivity forecasts which campaign will change the answer before you commit budget (Simulate), routes the work with owners and partners (Act), then measures predicted vs actual lift (Learn). The full loop, not half of it.
Why India-first?
Because the answer graph is local. Indian buyers ask in Hinglish and regional languages, and they decide on surfaces — Amazon Rufus, Flipkart, Blinkit — that global tools ignore. Our category depth and surface coverage run deepest in India. We expand market by market from there.
Are the numbers on this site real?
No — every metric in the worked examples is a labelled model output. Real Indian brands appear for context only, to demonstrate the mechanic; nothing here is a statement of fact about any brand. Real results appear in the proof block, and only once we have a genuine design-partner outcome to publish.
How do you handle our data?
Your content, campaigns and outcomes live in your own tenant, encrypted at rest and in transit, and are never used to improve another customer's results. SSO and full audit logs are available; SOC 2 Type II is in progress with a Q4 2026 target.
