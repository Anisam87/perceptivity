How we work.

Five principles for the team and the product. The ones we'll be embarrassed by if we ever forget.

· 01

Forecast before you spend.

Every meaningful action — ours or our customers' — must come with a forecast. Show your work. Confidence levels, expected lift, defensible math. Marketing decisions deserve the same rigour as engineering ones.

Product principle
· 02

Measure what you moved.

If we can't measure the lift, we shouldn't have made the claim. Predicted vs actual is a sacred number. The CFO is allowed in every conversation.

Operating principle
· 03

Respect the brand. Reject the spam.

Shaping AI perception is not gaming AI. We don't pump fake reviews, we don't farm citations, we don't manufacture authority. Anything we wouldn't be proud to show a journalist, we don't ship.

Ethics
· 04

Local context is not an afterthought.

Local publishers, buyer language, competitive sets and regulatory context — built in per market, not bolted on. We go market by market and earn depth; India is where ours runs deepest today.

Market principle
· 05

Diagnosis is half the job.

An insight nobody acts on is wasted compute. Every gap in our dashboard ends in an action with an owner. We will not be a tool that decorates the problem.

Product principle
· 06

One loop, looped forever.

Sense. Simulate. Act. Learn. Every cycle makes the next forecast sharper. Compounding is a marketing strategy, not just a financial one.
