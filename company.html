The method.

How we earn the right to a finding — and why a sharp buyer can trust it.

· 01 / Query model

We model how India actually asks.

8,000+ India-tuned query profiles span language, city tier, income band, price sensitivity, and voice vs typed — so what we track is your buyer's real question, not a generic English prompt.

· 02 / Multi-engine sampling

We sample across engines, repeatedly.

Answer share is tracked across 10+ AI engines and re-sampled over time, so a single lucky answer never becomes a finding and drift is caught as it happens.

· 03 / Predicted vs measured

The gap is the product.

Every modelled move is logged against the outcome we measure. We never hide the miss — the difference is exactly what sharpens the next estimate.

· 04 / Grounding, not gaming

We move answers by changing the source.

We publish structured, authoritative, machine-readable truth that engines ingest over weeks — never fake reviews, citation farms, or manufactured authority. If we wouldn't show it to a journalist, we don't ship it.
